Things to Do at Topsail Beach

Natural beauty abounds at Topsail Beach! The undisturbed, undeveloped barriar island and marsh system allows visitors a unique way to explore wildlife that is not possible in overdeveloped tourist areas. Loggerhead sea turtles, simmers, and terns nest here. Island tours are available to learn more about our wildlife.

With plentiful public beach access, there is a wealth of open space to enjoy the ocean. You can freely explore the beach with Electric Bike Rentals. Learn more about World War II history at the Missles and More Museum. Stretch your legs and get your heartrate up at the Topsail Beach Skating Rink. Settle into a pace that will help you rejuvenate and unwind.
